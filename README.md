# Jazz Improvisation App

## Project Vision

The goal of this Flutter mobile application is to provide a comprehensive platform for teaching jazz improvisation on the alto saxophone. By integrating a language learning model, we aim to transform the learning process into an engaging and intuitive experience where:

- **Licks are Words:** Each lick (a musical phrase used in improvisation) represents a word in our jazz vocabulary, enabling players to build their improvisational skills through practice and repetition.
- **Phrases are Sentences:** Combinations of licks form phrases, similar to how sentences are structured in language. This approach allows learners to create musical expressions and develop their personal style.

The app will leverage technology to offer personalized feedback and progression tracking, ensuring users can refine their skills over time. Through interactive lessons, challenges, and community engagement, we will cultivate a vibrant learning environment for aspiring jazz musicians.
